Triple Entry Journal 1 - The First Day

Quotation from the textLiterary TechniqueAnalysis

"In an otherwise unremarkable September morning, long before I learned to be ashamed of my mother"

Hyperbole

Jones uses hyperbole to show the main character's gratitude for her mother helping enroll her daughter into school using the words "unremarkable", "otherwise", and the word "ashamed". The word "otherwise" means "if al else fails", which is an example of  exaggeration when the completely avoidable does happen. Next, the word "unremarkable" used with "otherwise" is an example of hyperbole as the otherwise inevitably remarkable day is not remarkable, since she had a loss for words. Next, the word "ashamed" shows something that usually does not happen: being ashamed of a parent.
