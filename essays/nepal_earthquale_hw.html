My Take on the Earthquake in Nepal

My take on the Earthquake in Nepal is that we take a modified version of the "Keep Calm, Carry On" approach as we should carry on with our lives to help others, but to stop and help the people affected by the earthquake's aftermath the best that we can help them. The reason that we help those people without  stopping the course of our lives is to make sure that we carry on and we remember happened to help.
