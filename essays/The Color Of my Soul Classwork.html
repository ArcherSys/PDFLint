                 In The Color of My Soul, Alex helped Kira find her identity by having a small  dance that made her feel good inside. For example: "Kira turned up the volume, closed her asnd listened to the voices flowing with the flute  and the pounding drums. Alex sang  along, his voice low and melodic making sounds more than actual words that she could understand. She remebered his dancing  and suddenly felt the urge to jump and spin to the music. As if could see her thoughts, he said he would  teach her to dance, but she refused - immediately embarrassed..." (Hatter 140). The following quote tells the facts cold:  "he said he would teach her to dance" is exactly how Alex help Kira. The outcome comes after this happens: "she felt the urge to jump and spin to the music".  This qoute is how she rids the excuse she has used throught the book "but the truth is , I can't dance".
