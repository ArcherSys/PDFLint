Why we should team up

O Nadjah , please hark
I understand your pain
from the classes you have taken
As I have done the same 
and the grades looked almost mistaken
Nadjah, I know, there is a lot more
to that than before
However,  I want to see you again,
and I don’t wan’t to be left out alone
I understand Pre-calculus is hard as
I think I have the right answer,
but it always stabs me metaphorically
like Brutus did Caesar
everywhere except the upper body
As tthe answers are harder
to get down-packed than usual for me
Math in the SATs, class and other places
is my Achilles heel  
I understand your pain with AP US History
As I lost all my notes from the entire
School Year of 2014-2015 for that course
But now I try to keep my notes…  
For you, Nadjah, as I want you…
To Join my study group so that we can
blast the walls of complexity that
these assignments and courses erect
To stall us from success
For you ,  Nadjah...
 my notes will always be available to you
No matter what reason you need them for  
I will always come to see you in the lunchroom first
to study with you…
To break the walls that have tried to break us
To end the madness that caused us - 
to have the unthinkable of low grades
As our goal in this study group
is to get a grade that is good for
The principal’s list or high distinction honor roll
As we get uninvited together
Or we get to advance to the next grade together
If  you join my study group, then both of us will- 
Be victorious and have our names on that-
Powerpoint Slide for High Distinction… 
So will you study with me?
- Weldon Henson
