The Reason I Finish First

 The reason I finish first is my reason of my reason of 

writing this, as you my friend, I cannot just leave  you 

hanging as the reason I finish first is to keep my homework

quizzes, and tests in check, a quality that gives my supercomp-

uter-like brain and compassionate heart something to do 

something for the good of the world – to give someone a chance so

​they can have as much fun at prom as anyone else 

One has to have guts to have done so much work,

to have a good atitude towards taking the AP Language

to have done the SATs with so much eagerness

to have done so much of the academic work without 

a single problem,

To have done everything with an elastic heart,

a heart that would be able to stand anything

from the loss of many of my family members

to the loss of  a desktop computer, 

that almost crippled me when this school year started

 to missing the target SAT score for MIT by 140 points

and so  on and so on, nothing can damage my heart

as this heart was ridden of laziness and I

was taken out of my comfort zone to a place

where I gained strength at hand

to do better, to keep going, to never

let other people’s doubts get to your head

and to drown out those doubts

and make your dreams a real reality.

I didn’t just do the work ,

I did it with precision and rapidness 

like a buzz saw.  

The thing about what I do in all my classes is

I  have an accelerated processing unit in

my  brain that when properly regulated

thermodynamically, it gets the work done

fast and almost perfectly. Why is it done that way?

the only weakness I have in me is only activated  

if I don’t get enough water and overheat.

However, I never let my weaknesses take me down completely

as I strive to finish the work the best I can.  

I do this work so fast as a sacrifice to maintain  

and  harness the power of something

that is way more valuable to a girl

than a good singing voice : GRIT

I have 3 goals: To get a GPA than last year

using my operating system,

the second one we will save for last,

and  the  third is to conquer the SATs

    The third goal is my destiny to goto

Massechusetts Institute of Technology

 , and the fourth goal is to

make sure that I do one thing before  the school year ends:

Give The ArcherSys Operating System

the power to manage your Dropbox

using Python, Javascript ,  Ruby , Java (Android) , and PHP

I have been wanting to make my OS use python for months  

  I have a saying that goes through my head every day:

“You don’t start a fight – you finish it with peace in mind”​

I code now not just for myself  -

I code, I work hard at home and in school,

sacrifice many  things I used to have

,and I write and read to prepare for the SATs,

for learning new programming languages , all... 

for you, Nadjah.

Weldon Henson
