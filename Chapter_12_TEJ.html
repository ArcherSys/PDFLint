Quotation from the textAnalysis and Opinion of the textLevel 3 Question for the Future

"'Girl, sometimes I wonder," she said. "What am I doingat this newspaper?" Kira Smiled. she knew exactly what she meant. (Hatter 58)

What she means by that  is that Beverly wishes that she was working at a job with a better offer than the Fort Lewis Times. that means that Kira and Beverly were thinking the same thing. Well, here's the thing. you  have to not only do good in the subjects you love, but do it humbly, as there will be days where you might miss something or when projects go wrong. the key to success is to do what you can now to prevent what you don't want and cannot change later.

Explain how the author teaches us about procrastination's unpleasant effects through dialectic language.
